Your privacy

Your genetic information is protected

Your results belong to you and your care team. Genetic information is handled confidentially under HIPAA, and federal law adds specific protections for genetic test results.

A note on the Genetic Information Nondiscrimination Act (GINA)

GINA generally prevents health insurers and employers from using your genetic information against you.

GINA's protections apply to health insurance and employment. They do not extend to life, disability, or long-term-care insurance. If you have questions about how testing could affect those, ask your provider or the GeneID Patient Support team.
